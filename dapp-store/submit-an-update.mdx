## 1. Navigate to the Publisher Portal

Log into the [Solana dApp Publisher Portal](https://publish.solanamobile.com) and find your published app in the sidebar.

## 2. Update your app details

Edit your app details (e.g. name, description, preview assets) on the **"Details"** page.

<Note>
**NOTE**

Changes only appear in the dApp Store after a new release version is approved.

</Note>

  <img src="/images/static/publishing_portal/update-dapp-details.png" />
</Frame>

## 3. Submit an updated release version

Navigate to your app's **"Home"** screen and press **"New Version"**.

### Submit an existing APK

If you only want to make a change to your app details and do not want to submit a new APK build, then select
the **"use existing APK"** option.

### Submit an updated APK

If you want to submit a new APK, ensure:

- You're submitting from the same publisher wallet used for the app's original submission.
- The APK is signed with the same Android signing key you used for your initial release.
- The **package name** matches your original submission.
- The `versionName` and `versionCode` are incremented from your previous release.
- The "What's new" section is filled out. This section will be displayed to users in your dApp Store listing page.

<Warning>
**WARNING**

An update must match your original submission exactly - the same publisher wallet, package name, and signing certificate. A mismatch in any of these is rejected and can't be recovered on that submission; you'll need to resubmit from the original wallet, package name, and signing key.

</Warning>

After you've completed the upload, press **"Submit"**. You'll be prompted to sign messages and transactions for uploading to the storage provider and minting your release NFT.

## After submission

Once submitted, the new release automatically enters the app review queue and goes live once approved. Releases are reviewed in submission order, typically within **3-5 business days**, with the result sent to your **Contact Email**.

Need to check on a submission or have a publishing question? See the [dApp Store Support](/dapp-store/support) page for review status inquiries and how to reach Solana Mobile on Discord.
