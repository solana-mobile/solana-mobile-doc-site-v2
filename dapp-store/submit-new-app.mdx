## Before you begin

Ensure you have:

- A release-ready APK, [signed with your release key](/dapp-store/build-and-sign-an-apk) and tested to install and launch on a supported device. Keep this key safe - every future update must be signed with the same one.
- An original app you built - copied, repackaged, or clone/template apps are rejected during review.
- App metadata prepared (name, description, screenshots, icon).
- A browser extension wallet (e.g. Backpack, Jupiter, Phantom, Solflare) funded with at least ~0.1 SOL - hardware wallets aren't supported.
- Reviewed the [Publisher Policy](/dapp-store/publisher-policy) and [Developer Agreement](/dapp-store/agreement).

## 1. Sign up for a publisher account

- Navigate to the [Publisher Portal](https://publish.solanamobile.com) and sign up for an account.
- Fill out your publisher profile details and complete identity verification (KYC/KYB).

<Note>
**NOTE**

KYC/KYB verification must be completed before you can submit a new app or publish an update to an existing app.

</Note>

## 2. Connect your publisher wallet

- Connect a Solana wallet at the top left of the page. This will be your _publisher wallet_.
- Ensure the publisher wallet has sufficient SOL (at least ~0.1 SOL) to cover transaction fees and storage provider upload costs.

<Warning>
**WARNING**

Publisher wallets are assigned per dApp, not per account - the wallet you connect here is permanently bound to this app and required for every future submission. Do not lose access to it, or you won't be able to publish updates.

</Warning>

## 3. Add your dApp details

In the Publisher Portal, press **"Add a dApp"**, then select **"New dApp"**. A form will open where you can enter your app's details.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/static/publishing_portal/new-dapp-details.png" />
</Frame>

Fill out the full listing, including:

- **dApp Name**, **Subtitle**, and **Description**.
- **Package Name** in reverse domain format (e.g. `com.example.app`). This permanently identifies your dApp and must stay the same across all future updates.
- **dApp Icon** (512×512px) and **Banner** feature graphic (1200×600px) - both required.

Your name, icon, screenshots, and description should accurately represent the app users will install.

### Contact details

Both fields are required and must be valid, actively monitored email addresses - app review correspondence is sent here.

- **Contact Email** - primary contact for the Solana dApp Store.
- **Support Email** - receives user support requests.

### Compliance links

- **Terms of Use** and **Privacy Policy** - both required. Each must link to a dedicated page that hosts the actual End User Agreement / Privacy Policy (e.g. `https://yourdomain.com/privacy-policy`).
- **App Website** - a live, public site for your app, or your studio/publisher site if you publish multiple apps. It must load and clearly relate to your app; `localhost`, parked, placeholder, or unreachable URLs are rejected.

<Warning>
**WARNING**

Terms of Use and Privacy Policy links are verified during app review. Each must be its own dedicated page that loads and hosts the actual policy. The following are **rejected**:

- **Root domain URLs** - e.g. `https://yourdomain.com/`. The URL must include a path beyond the root, such as `https://yourdomain.com/terms`.
- **Anchor-only links** - e.g. `https://yourdomain.com/#policy`. The policy must exist on its own dedicated page, not as a section of another page.
- **Combined pages** - e.g. one `https://yourdomain.com/legal` URL used for both. Terms of Use and Privacy Policy must be two separate, dedicated pages.
- **Unreachable pages** - links that return an error or 404, or redirect back to the root domain.
- **Unreadable pages** - the policy text must be legible. Near-zero contrast (e.g. black text on a black background) is rejected as an accessibility violation.

</Warning>

Once every required field is complete, the **Save** button in the top-right turns green. These details can be changed before submission and updated in later submissions.

## 4. Mint your dApp

On the **Home** page, press **"Mint App"** to begin the release flow.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/static/publishing_portal/home-mint-app.png" />
</Frame>

Review your app's NFT details, then press **"Complete NFT Minting"** and confirm the transaction.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/static/publishing_portal/app-minting.png" />
</Frame>

## 5. Create your first release

Press **"New Version"** in the top-right and upload the production build you intend to ship and have tested end-to-end.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/static/publishing_portal/home-no-releases.png" />
</Frame>

<Note>
**NOTE**

Reviewers install and run your app, so it must launch and its core features must work on a supported device, and it should request only the Android permissions it actually uses. It must deliver real in-app functionality rather than only redirecting users to an external site - web apps are supported when packaged as an Android app, see [Publishing a Web App](/recipes/general/publishing-a-web-app).

</Note>

Add your initial release notes in the box below, then press **"Submit"**. This uploads your APK to the storage provider and prepares the minting of your first release. You'll be prompted to sign multiple messages and transactions to complete the upload and minting.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/static/publishing_portal/dapp-apk-upload.png" />
</Frame>

<Note>
**NOTE**

Approve every signing request - skipping any can leave assets missing from your submission.

</Note>

<Check>**Congratulations on submitting your first app! 🎉**</Check>

## After submission

Once submitted, your app automatically enters the app review queue and goes live under an appropriate category once approved. Apps are reviewed in submission order, typically within **3-5 business days**, with the result sent to your **Contact Email**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/static/publishing_portal/release-in-review.png" />
</Frame>

Need to check on a submission or have a publishing question? See the [dApp Store Support](/dapp-store/support) page for review status inquiries and how to reach Solana Mobile on Discord.
